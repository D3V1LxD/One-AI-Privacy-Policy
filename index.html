Privacy Policy for One AI

Last Updated: May 24, 2025

Welcome to One AI (the "Bot"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our Bot on the Telegram platform. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the Bot.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Bot, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Telegram User ID: Your unique Telegram identifier is collected automatically when you interact with the Bot.
  • Messages and Prompts: The text messages, questions, and prompts you send to the Bot are processed to provide you with responses from the selected AI models.
  • AI Model Preference: We store your choice of AI model (e.g., "gpt4.1", "gptmini") to personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data:
    • Question Counts: We track the number of questions you ask to manage daily usage limits for non-paid users.
    • Timestamps: We record timestamps related to your usage for limit reset purposes.
  • User Status: We store whether you are a "Regular" or "Paid" user to manage access to features.
  • Interaction Data: We may collect data about your interactions with the Botâ€™s commands (e.g., `/mystatus`, `/setmodel`).

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To Operate the Bot: To receive your messages, process them through your selected AI model, and deliver responses.
  • To Manage User Accounts: To enforce usage limits, manage paid subscriptions, and store your model preferences.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To remember your chosen AI model for subsequent interactions.
  • To Communicate with You: The Bot Admin may use the broadcast feature to send important updates or announcements to all users.
  • To Improve the Bot: We may analyze usage patterns (in an aggregated and anonymized manner where possible) to improve the Bot's functionality and user experience.
  • To Provide Support: To assist you with any issues or inquiries you may have regarding the Bot.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • Third-Party AI APIs: Your messages and prompts are sent to third-party AI service providers (e.g., the service hosting models like "openai/gpt-4.1", "microsoft/MAI-DS-R1", etc., via the Azure/GitHub Inference endpoint) to generate responses. These services have their own privacy policies, which you are encouraged to review. We are not responsible for the data handling practices of these third-party AI providers.
  • Bot Administrator: The Bot administrator (@Beluga_Tele or designated personnel) has access to the user data (including User IDs, usage counts, paid status, model preferences) stored for the purpose of managing the Bot, providing support, and enforcing terms.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend our rights or property, or in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of the Bot or the public.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • User Data File (`users.json`): Information such as your Telegram User ID, paid status, question count, last reset timestamp, and current model preference is stored in a JSON file on the server where the Bot is hosted.
  • Conversation History: The Bot maintains a history of your current conversation (messages from you and the AI) in its memory to provide contextual responses. This history is reset when you use the `/newchat` command or when a new session begins.
  • Security Measures: We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include:

  • Access: You can view some of your data (like usage status and Telegram ID) using the `/mystatus` command.
  • Correction: If you believe any information we hold about you is incorrect, you can contact the Bot administrator.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your user data by contacting the Bot administrator. Please note that deleting your data will remove your usage history and preferences.
  • Stop Using the Bot: You can stop using the Bot at any time by simply ceasing interaction or blocking the Bot on Telegram.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information collected as described in this Privacy Policy for as long as you use the Bot, or as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws or regulations. User-specific data stored in `users.json` will be retained until you request its deletion or the Bot administrator deems it necessary to remove inactive user data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Bot (e.g., via a broadcast message or a link in the `/about` command) and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
